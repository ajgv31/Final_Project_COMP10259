The Goliath Grouper is one of the largest reef fish in the world, known for its impressive size and distinctive appearance. Found in the Western Atlantic, this species is critical to marine ecosystems but is facing serious threats.The goliath grouper is the largest grouper species in the Atlantic Ocean weighing up to 800 pounds. They were once so overfished in the southeastern United States, they were considered for listing under the Endangered Species Act.
